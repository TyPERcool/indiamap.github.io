Punjab

Punjab is a state located in the northwestern part of India. It is bordered by the Indian states of Jammu and Kashmir to the north, Himachal Pradesh to the northeast, Haryana to the south, and Rajasthan to the west. The state also shares an international border with Pakistan. The capital of Punjab is Chandigarh, which is a shared capital with the neighboring state of Haryana.

   Punjab is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. It is often referred to as the "Land of Five Rivers" due to its location along the Beas, Chenab, Jhelum, Ravi, and Sutlej rivers. The state is renowned for its vibrant culture, including traditional music, dance, and cuisine. Punjab's agricultural landscape is marked by extensive wheat and rice cultivation, making it one of India's most productive agricultural regions.

   The state's economy is largely based on agriculture, but it also has a growing industrial sector. Punjab is known for its food processing, textile, and manufacturing industries. The state’s rich traditions and historical significance make it an important region in India's northern region.

Punjab is divided into 23 districts. Each district has its own unique attributes and contributes to the state’s overall diversity.
