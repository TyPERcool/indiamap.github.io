Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state located in the northwestern part of India, known for its vibrant culture, historic forts, and desert landscapes. It is bordered by Punjab to the north, Haryana and Uttar Pradesh to the east, Madhya Pradesh to the southeast, Gujarat to the southwest, and shares an international border with Pakistan to the west. The capital of Rajasthan is Jaipur, which is also known as the "Pink City" due to its distinctive pink-colored buildings.

   Rajasthan is famous for its royal heritage, including grand palaces, majestic forts, and colorful festivals. The Thar Desert, which spans much of the state, adds to its unique charm. Rajasthan's cultural traditions are reflected in its folk music, dance, and cuisine, including dishes like dal bati churma and gatte ki sabzi.
