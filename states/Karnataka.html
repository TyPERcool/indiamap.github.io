Karnataka

Karnataka is a state located in the southwestern region of India. It is bordered by Goa to the northwest, Maharashtra to the north, Andhra Pradesh to the east, Tamil Nadu to the southeast, and Kerala to the west. The state’s capital is Bengaluru, which is also the largest city and a major tech hub known for its IT industry and cosmopolitan culture.

   Karnataka is renowned for its diverse geography, which includes coastal regions, forests, hills, and plateaus. The state has a rich cultural heritage, with a blend of traditional music, dance, and art forms. It is also famous for its historical sites, such as the ruins of Hampi and the heritage city of Mysore.

   The state’s economy is driven by sectors such as information technology, biotechnology, manufacturing, and agriculture. Karnataka is also known for its production of coffee, silk, and various minerals. The state's vibrant cultural scene and economic significance make it an important region in India.



Districts in Karnataka

Karnataka is divided into 31 districts. These districts are known for their diverse landscapes and cultural significance.
