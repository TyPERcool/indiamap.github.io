Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is a state located in the southern part of India, bordered by Karnataka to the northwest, Andhra Pradesh to the north, and Kerala to the west. It also has a coastline along the Bay of Bengal to the east. The state’s capital is Chennai, which is one of India's largest cities and a major cultural and economic hub.

   Tamil Nadu is known for its rich cultural heritage, including its classical dance forms such as Bharatanatyam, and its historic temples and monuments. The state is renowned for its Dravidian-style architecture, with temples like the Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur and the Meenakshi Temple in Madurai being notable examples.

   The economy of Tamil Nadu is diverse, with significant contributions from industries such as automobile manufacturing, textiles, and information technology. The state is also a major agricultural producer, with crops like rice, sugarcane, and cotton being prominent. Tamil Nadu's vibrant cultural traditions, scenic landscapes, and economic vitality make it an important state in India's southern region.
