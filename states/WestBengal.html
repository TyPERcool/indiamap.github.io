West Bengal

West Bengal is a state located in the eastern part of India. It is bordered by the Indian states of Bihar to the west, Jharkhand to the southwest, and Odisha to the south. To the north, it shares a border with Nepal and Bhutan, and to the east, it is adjacent to Bangladesh. The capital of West Bengal is Kolkata, which is the largest city in the state.

   West Bengal is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant festivals, and diverse landscapes. The state is renowned for its colonial architecture, bustling markets, and cultural institutions such as the Indian Museum and Victoria Memorial. The cultural diversity is reflected in its festivals, including Durga Puja, which is celebrated with great enthusiasm.

   The economy of West Bengal is diverse, with major contributions from agriculture, manufacturing, and services. Key industries include textiles, information technology, and tea production.
