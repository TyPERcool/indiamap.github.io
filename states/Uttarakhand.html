Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a state located in the northern part of India, known for its stunning natural landscapes, including the Himalayas and numerous rivers. It is bordered by the Indian states of Uttar Pradesh to the south, Haryana to the west, and Himachal Pradesh to the northwest. The state also shares an international border with China to the north. The capital of Uttarakhand is Dehradun, and the largest city is Haridwar.

   Uttarakhand is renowned for its scenic beauty and is a popular destination for tourism and pilgrimage.    The state's economy is primarily driven by tourism, agriculture, and hydroelectric power. Uttarakhand's diverse natural resources and cultural heritage make it an important and unique state in India.
